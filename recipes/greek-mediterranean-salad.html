My arranged Greek/Mediterranean salad

Naming this one was difficult. I really wanted a jazzed up salad one day and decided to make it sort of a mix of two cuisines. There's a big dollop of baba ganoush in the center to really meld the flavors together. A very well-known Greek ingredient is olive, but I'm not a big fan of olives. However, you can absolutely add them to your salad.

What you'll need:

  • 3 oz Green leafy vegetable of your choice
  • 2 oz Halved cherry tomatoes
  • 2 oz Jarred marinated artichoke hearts
  • 1 oz King's Falafel prepared baba ganoush
  • 1/2 oz (Or desired amount) Thinly sliced red onion
  • 1/4 oz Black Olives (Optional)
  • 1/4 c Canned, drained garbanzo beans
  • 1/4 c Reduced-fat feta cheese
  • 1-2 TB Dressing of your choice

Directions

  1. Wash and prep your produce. Thinly slice red onions, half cherry tomatoes and set aside. Place your salad greens in a bowl
  2. Arrange your toppings over the salad greens as desired. I made an alternating pattern between ingredients as the picture above.
  3. Place the baba ganoush in the center of your salad.
  4. Drizzle your favorite dressing over this salad and enjoy!.
