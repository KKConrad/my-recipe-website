Bacon Asparagus Bundles

A friend of mine made these for a Thanksgiving get-together last year and I was absolutely hooked. This certainly deserves a 10/10 rating and what makes it even more outstanding is that it's so incredibly simple! These Bacon Aparagus Bundles make for excellent appetizers or even an elegant side dish for your favorite main course.

What you'll need:

  • 2 lb Fresh asparagus, ends trimmed.
  • 12 Slices of thick bacon
  • 1/2 c Light brown sugar
  • 1/2 c (1 stick) Unsalted butter
  • 1 T Soy sauce
  • 1/2 t Garlic salt
  • 1/4 t freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Divide the asparagus spears into 12 bundles. Carefully wrap 1 piece of bacon around each bundle, starting about 1/2 inch from the bottom of the tips. Secure the bacon-wrapped spears with a toothpick. Lay the bundles in a low-sided casserole dish.
  3. In a medium saucepan, combine the brown sugar, butter, soy sauce, garlic salt and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil. Pour the hot sugar mixture over the asparagus bundles.
  4. Transfer the dish to the oven and roast until the spears have begun to wilt and the bacon looks fully cooked, about 25 minutes. Remove the toothpicks before serving.
